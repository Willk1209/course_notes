## Probability and Counting

- **A sample space** is the set of all possible outcomes of an experiment.
- **An event** is a subset of the sample space. 



- Binomial coefficient 
  $$
  \tbinom{n}{k} = \frac{n!}{(n-k)!k!}
  $$
